## 🎯 How Users Use It

```
1. Click "Download Template"
   ↓
2. Template downloads (language-specific)
   ↓
3. Fill in their team's emails & names in Excel
   ↓
4. Click "Choose File"
   ↓
5. Select their Excel file
   ↓
6. System processes & validates
   ↓
7. Results shown:
   "3 added · 1 invalid · 0 duplicates"
   ↓
8. Continue with onboarding
```
